## Troubleshooting

### "Can't find INTERNAL TESTING"
- Make sure you're in the **TestFlight** tab
- Look in the **left sidebar** under "Testers"
- If missing, you may need to enable it in App Store Connect settings

### "No email invite received"
- Wait 5-10 minutes (can take time)
- Check spam/junk folder
- Open TestFlight app directly - app may appear without email
- Verify email matches your Apple ID: `brogdon.lance@gmail.com`

### "Build not available"
